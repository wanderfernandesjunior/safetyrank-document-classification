ALERT 15-01 

 

 

UNINTENTIONAL UNLATCHING OF AN AIR OPERATED ELEVATOR 

WHAT HAPPENED: 
While the rig was operating using “single joint drill pipe” operations, the 350 Ton air operated elevator was unintentionally opened by 
the Driller. This resulted in a single joint of drill pipe dropped onto the Drill Floor from a height of +/- 3 meters before coming to rest on 
the Derrick structure. 
 
WHAT CAUSED IT: 
•  Skill based (training & competency of person at controls) Mistake 
•  Cyberbase/air operated elevator system(s) can open at any given time (double click left joy stick button).
