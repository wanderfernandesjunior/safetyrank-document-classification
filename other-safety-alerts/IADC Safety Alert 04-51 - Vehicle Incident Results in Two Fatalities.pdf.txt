ALERT 04 – 51 
 

VEHICLE INCIDENT RESULTS IN TWO FATALITIES 

 
WHAT HAPPENED: 
 
A mechanic who was on his time off was driving a company pickup.  The driver picked up six non-company 
personnel.  Two of the passengers rode in the pickup cab and four of the passengers rode in the pickup bed.  
The driver swerved to miss a pothole and rolled the pickup, killing two passengers, injuring himself and the 
other passengers.  Potential was there for additional fatalities. 

WHAT CAUSED IT: 
 
The mechanic disregarded the company vehicle policy with regard to carrying non-work related passengers.  
He also disregarded the company policy on the proper use of seatbelts and transporting personnel in the bed 
of the truck.  The facts surrounding the incident indicate a lack of adequate training and supervision. 
 
CORRECTIVE ACTIONS:  To address this incident, this company reminded company personnel: 
 
Procedures  are  in  place  for  the  safety  and  well  being  of  company  personnel  and  the  general  public.  
Disregarding policy can place people at risk of injury or death.  

 

 

Message:   
 
Increased  efforts  must  be  put  forth  to  train  personnel  with  regard  to  company  vehicle  policies,  and  ensure 
that personnel follow company vehicle policies through observation.  Drivers of company vehicles must be 
evaluated and held to strict adherence to company policy. 
 

The Corrective Actions stated in this alert are one company’s attempts to address the incident,  

and do not necessarily reflect the position of IADC or the IADC HSE Committee.
