Safety Alert 

        From the International Association of Drilling Contractors 

 
ALERT 07 – 33 
 

LACK OF ADEQUATE REST AND ATTENTION RESULTING  

IN AN LTI (RIG MOVE) 

 
WHAT HAPPENED: 
 
After  completing  75%  of  a  rig  move  operation,  the  Senior  Mechanic  (IP)  was  instructed  by  the  Senior 
Toolpusher to connect the mud pump engine's air and diesel lines to arrange for rig acceptance.  After he 
connected  the  air  and  diesel  lines  and  the  air  supply  valve  was  opened  it  appeared  that  there  was  an  air 
leak.   The injured stopped to check the leaking air line and his left foot was twisted and he lost his balance 
and fell to the ground.   
 
WHAT CAUSED IT: 
 
• 
•  Lack of due care and attention due to IP was physically after working for 22 hours with only 2 1/2 hours 

Incident took place in day no. 13 of the IP's shift. 

rest/sleep. 

 
CORRECTIVE ACTIONS:  To address this incident, this company did the following: 
 
•  All workers should have sufficient sleep between shifts. 
•  More care and effort to be taken while rig move time. 
•  Declare the incident to all rig crews during pre –tour and weekly HSE meeting including off shift crews. 
•  Pre-tour and pre- job safety meetings during rig moves to include the risk related slips, trips, step on/off 

and falls. 

 
 

The Corrective Actions stated in this alert are one company’s attempts to address the incident,  

and do not necessarily reflect the position of IADC or the IADC HSE Committee.
