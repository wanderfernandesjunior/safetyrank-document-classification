Safety Alert 

From the International Association of Drilling Contractors

 

 
ALERT 03 – 13 
 

IMPROPER POSITION OF HELPER RESULTS IN 

LOST TIME INCIDENT 

 
WHAT HAPPENED: 
 
Forklift operator and helper are trying to get this kelly (pipe) into the casing.  Helper is giving hand signals to 
driver.  The pipe rolled off the forks and hit the helper’s foot.  The helper’s foot and ankle were broken.  His 
colleagues had to lift the pipe off his foot.  He will be off work for at least 4 weeks. 
WHAT CAUSED IT: 
 
•  Wrong position of the helper   
•  Pipe rolled off the forks 
•  Driver and helper had not “seen” the hazard/danger 
 
 
CORRECTIVE ACTIONS:   

Helper directing fork lift operator. 

This company posed the following questions to their personnel:      
 
• 
• 

How could this incident have been prevented?  
Is a forklift the right tool for this job? Are there situations like this one in your work? Are you aware of the 
Hazard/Danger of these type of operations?

Helper decided to use foot to guide kelly.    

 

 

 
Kelly sides off of forks and onto  

helper’s foot. 

    

The Corrective Actions stated in this alert are one company’s attempts to address the incident, and do not 

necessarily reflect the position of IADC or the IADC HSE Committee.
