ALERT 03 – 29 
 

SANDBLASTING ACTIVITY RESULTS IN AN INJECTION INJURY 

 
WHAT HAPPENED: 
 
While sandblasting in the mud pits, an employee lost control of a sandblasting nozzle, resulting in an injection 
of sand particles into his left forearm. 
 
WHAT CAUSED IT: 
 
While repositioning in the mud pits, the employee had released the trigger on the blasting nozzle.  Before the 
pressure  on  the  nozzle  had  completely  bled  down  the  employee  re-engaged  the  nozzle  trigger.    The  nozzle 
pressured up faster than the employee had expected.  The employee did not have a good grip on the blasting 
nozzle.    As  a  result  the  employee  lost  control  of  the  nozzle  and  swept  it  across  his  left  arm  causing  the 
injection injury. 
 
CORRECTIVE ACTIONS:  To address this incident, this company instructed rig supervisory personnel:   
 
•  Review with all crewmembers the proper procedures for sandblasting and be sure that they are properly 

•  Review all hazards associated with this operation and the methods to eliminate them. 
•  Thoroughly inspect all blasting equipment for damage, malfunction and proper safety guards. 
•  Put into service additional protective equipment such as leather jackets and gloves to complement 

trained. 

standard PPE.
