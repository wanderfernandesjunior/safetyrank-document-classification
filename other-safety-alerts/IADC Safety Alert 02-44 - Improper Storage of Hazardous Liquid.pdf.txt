ALERT 02 – 44  
 

IMPROPER STORAGE OF HAZARDOUS LIQUID 

 
WHAT HAPPENED: 
 
An incident occurred on a rig in which a local employee drank methanol that was being stored in a soft drink 
bottle  in  the  mechanic  shop  on  the  rig.    The  employee  was  unaware  that  the  container  contained  methanol.  
He immediately began to vomit and was taken to the rig clinic where he was treated.   
 
WHAT CAUSED IT: 
 
This incident occurred because hazardous material was being kept in an unlabeled container.   
 
CORRECTIVE ACTIONS:  To address this incident, this company issued the following directive to all 
rigs:   
 
•  ALL  CONTAINERS  USED  FOR  HAZARDOUS  MATERIAL  SHALL  BE  LABELED  AS  HAZARDOUS 

MATERIAL.  The name of the contents shall be clearly visible on the container.  

Inspect all areas on your rig to ensure all containers are properly labeled.
